Key Points to Remember:

  • Rolling 1: Ends your turn, and you lose your turn points.
  • Rolling any other number: Adds to your turn total; you can roll again or hold.
  • Hold: Adds your turn total to your score, and it's the next player's turn.
  • Winning: The first player to reach 100 points wins.

To make moves:

Click 'New Game,' 'Roll Dice,' or 'Hold,' or use these commands:

  • N: New game
  • R: Roll dice and add to your turn total
  • H: End your turn and add to your score
  • Escape: Close Instructions
